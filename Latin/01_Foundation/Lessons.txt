	Section: Foundation
	Lesson 1: First Declension Nouns (Nominative and Accusative)
	- new 1st decl nouns (30): amīca, aqua, aquila, bestia, casa, cēna,
		columba, discipula, fābula, familia, fēmina, fīlia,
		herba, īnsula, lūna, magistra, mēnsa, patria, pecūnia, porta, puella,
		rāna, rosa, schōla, silva, stēlla, terra, vacca, via
	- special nouns: agricola, nauta, pīrāta, poēta, aurīga
	- no verbs, et, aut, et..et, aut..aut, nec, nec..nec

	Lesson 2: First Conjugation (-āre verbs) Present Tense Active singular"
	- new 1st conj verbs (30): adorāre, amāre, ambulāre, cantāre, clamāre,
		cūrāre, errāre, exspectāre, habitāre, intrāre,
		iuvāre, labōrāre, laudāre, lavāre, līberāre, nārrāre, nāvigāre, optāre,
		ōrāre, parāre, portāre,	pugnāre, rogāre, saltāre, salūtāre, servāre,
		spectāre, temptāre, vexāre, vocāre
	- additional verbs: esse, habēre, dare, stāre

	Lesson 3: First Conjugation (-āre verbs) Present Tense Active plural
	- new adverbs: bene, crās, hodiē, lente,
		male, mox, nunc, saepe, semper, statim, subitō, celeriter

	Lesson 4: First Declension Nouns (Dative)
	- new 1s decl nouns (30): ancilla, causa, cūra, cūria, fāma, fossa,
		fuga, grātia, hora, iniūria, īra, lacrima, lingua, littera,
		machina, māteria, mora, nātūra, opera, penna, poena, rēgīna, sagitta,
		spēlunca, taberna, tenebra, toga, umbra, fēria
	- sed, -que, nōn, quoque
	- special: fīlia - fīliābus
	- additional verbs: posse
	- special case: poenam dare 

	Lesson 5: Second Declension Masculine Nouns
	- new 2nd decl masc nouns (30): ager, amīcus, animus,
		campus, captīvus, deus, dominus, equus, fīlius,
		fluvius, hortus, liber, locus, lupus, marītus, modus, mūrus, numerus,
		nuntius, oculus, populus, puer, servus, socius, verus, victus, vir
	- special nouns: pīnus, morus, alvus, humus, laurus
	- note: bonatical names are feminine 

	Lesson 6: Ablative Case
	- new 2nd decl masc nouns (30): annus, asinus, barbarus, carrus, cervus,
		cibus, circus, cōlus, coquus, digitus, faber, gladius,
		humerus, inimīcus, lūdus, magister, medicus, morbus, nāsus,
		pīlus, ramus, rīvus, saccus, scūtum, sonus, taurus, ventus, vīcus
	- Ablative: in, cum, ab, ex, sub (location/means)
	- Accusative: in, ad, sub (motion toward)
	- additional verbs: velle
	- special: fīlia - fīliābus

	Lesson 7: Second Declension Neuter Nouns
	- new 2nd decl neuter nouns (30): argentum, argumentum, aurum, auxilium,
		baculum, balneum, bellum, bracchium, caelum, cōnsilium, corpus, dōnum,
		exemplum, factum, ferrum, gaudium, imperium, lumen, oppidum, ōvum,
		perīculum, praemium, regnum, sacrum, stagnum, tēctum, templum,
		verbum, vīnum, saxum
	- nihil

	Lesson 8: Adjectives
	- new adjectives (30): albus, altus, blandus, bonus, candidus,
		clarus, durus, ferus, fidus, grātus, iratus, laetus, longus, magnus,
		malus, medius, miser, multus, niger, novus, parvus, paucus, pulcher,
		ruber, sacer, saevus, sanctus, sollicitus
	- adjective-noun agreement 
	- additional verbs: nolle
	- special: pulcher
	 - nam

	Lesson 9: Second Conjugation (-ēre verbs) Present Tense Active 
	- new 2nd conj verbs (30): ardēre, augēre, cavēre, complēre, dēbēre,
		dēlēre, docēre, fervēre, flēre, fovēre, habēre, iacēre, manēre, monēre,
		movēre, patēre, respondēre, ridēre, sedēre, silēre, splendēre, tacēre,
		tenēre, terrēre, timēre, valēre, vidēre, vigēre
	- si, quia

	Lesson 10: Genitive Case (Possession)
	 - new 2nd decl neuter nouns (30): acetum, arvum, atrium,
	 	colloquium, corium, damnum, fatum, forum, incendium,
		ingenium, initium, iūdicium, iugum, lignum, malum,
		mendacium, negōtium, nūntium, odium, officium, ōtium, pavimentum,
		praeceptum, pretium, proelium, sepulcrum, signum, spatium, subsidium, vestīgium
	 - plural only: arma, castra
	 - additional verbs: īre
	Lesson 11: Review
