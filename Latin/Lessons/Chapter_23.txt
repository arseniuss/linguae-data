# Delve into ut clauses for expressing purpose ("ut veniam" - so that I may
# come) and result ("tam magnus ut...").
# Expand vocabulary for linking clauses with tantus, tam, ita.

# Ut (that, in order to) – Used to express purpose (e.g., "ut veniat" – so that
# he may come).
# Quod (that) – Used in result clauses (e.g., "tam magnus quod...").
# Itaque (therefore) – Used to introduce a result or consequence.
# Tam, tantus, sic (so, so great, thus) – Used to indicate the extent of
# something in result clauses.
